🔄 Pull from Google Sheet → Dashboard

Pulls data from your Google Sheet and loads it into the dashboard. This will replace your current local data. Use this when you have data in the sheet that's not on this device.

How two-way sync works:
• Auto-sync ON → every new bill, expense, stock change pushes to sheet instantly
• Push All → sends everything from dashboard to sheet (overwrites sheet)
• Pull from Sheet → loads sheet data into dashboard (overwrites local)
• Use Pull when switching devices or restoring data from sheet
